## How it works

<CardGrid stagger>
  <Card title="Borrow SOL" icon="rocket">
    Get a SOL loan to launch your token on pump.fun. Choose your loan amount (0.1–10 SOL) and duration (1–24 hours).
  </Card>
  <Card title="Launch Tokens" icon="star">
    Moono automatically creates your token on pump.fun and makes the initial buy — all in a single transaction.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Provide Liquidity" icon="seti:liquid">
    Supply SOL to the liquidity pool, choose your risk tier, and earn interest from borrowers.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Transparent Economics" icon="open-book">
    All fees and interest rates are computed on-chain. No hidden costs — everything is verifiable in the smart contract.
  </Card>
